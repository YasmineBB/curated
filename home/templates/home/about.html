About curated

Who are we?

We are curated. We love art, we love artists. And we want everyone to enjoy the beautiful work of talented artists as much as we do.

Our mission is to provide the platform to connect customers with artists.

We think that art is important, and that everyone should have the opportunity to incorporate it into their lives, fitting their own unique style. We platform the artist as much as the artwork.
